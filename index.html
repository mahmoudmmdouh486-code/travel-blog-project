Destinations

Paris

Stroll along the Seine, browse vintage bookstalls, and savor a flaky croissant in the shadow of Notre‑Dame.

  • Buy a carnet of metro tickets to save time.
  • Visit the Louvre on a weekday morning.
  • Pack a picnic for sunset at the Trocadéro.

Extra Paris Tips:

  • Try a river cruise at sunset for unique city views.
  • Explore Montmartre’s art scene and street performers.
  • Sample local cheeses at a fromagerie.

Tokyo

Sip matcha in quiet gardens, then dive into the electric buzz of Shibuya and Akihabara.

  • Get a Suica/PASMO card for easy transit.
  • Try an omakase lunch for better value.
  • Visit shrines early to avoid crowds.

Extra Tokyo Tips:

  • Visit Tsukiji Outer Market for fresh sushi breakfast.
  • Relax in an onsen (hot spring bath) for a local experience.
  • Shop for unique souvenirs in Harajuku.

Top 5 Travel Tips

  1. Pack layers and a compact rain jacket.
  2. Make digital + paper copies of documents.
  3. Keep a small first‑aid kit handy.
  4. Learn hello/thank you in the local language.
  5. Leave space in your schedule for serendipity.

Note: Prices and availability change—always double‑check before booking.
